Dance World Cup Nigeria 2026 Qualifier - Key Information
Overview
This is Nigeria's official qualifier for the Dance World Cup Finals, where dancers showcase their talent on the international stage.
Important Dates

Registeration Start for Qualifier Event: December 1, 2025
Registration Deadline for Qualifier Event: January 31, 2026
Qualifier Event Date: February 14, 2026
Finals Location: Dublin, July 8 - 18, 2026

Categories and Entry Fees (per participant)

Solo: ₦10,704 (€6) per dancer
Duet/Trio: ₦9,812 (€5.50) per dancer
Small Group (4-9 dancers): ₦8,920 (€5) per dancer
Large Group (10+ dancers): ₦8,028 (€4.50) per dancer
Spectator Admission: ₦3,568 (€2) per person

Dance Genres Included

Ballet & Neo Classical
Modern/Lyrical & Contemporary
Jazz/Show-Dance
Hip-Hop/Street Dance & Commercial
National Dance
Step Dance & Song and Dance

Eligibility and Registration Requirements

All registrations must be submitted through an official dance school
Dance schools register their dancers on the DWC event system
DWC/IDN Membership required for all participants

Judging and Scoring

Judging Panel: International DWC judges and local judges (international judges outnumber local judges)
Minimum qualifying score: 70 points (required to qualify for DWC Finals)
Scoring criteria: Technique, Performance and Expression, Choreography, Musicality and Timing
Judges' decisions are final and cannot be appealed

Awards
Medal/Certificate Distribution by Score:

Gold: 90 and above
Silver: 80-89
Bronze: 70-79

Special Awards for Winners

Solo & Duet/Trio Winners: Free entry to the Finals for the winning dances
Small & Large Group Winners: Voucher towards entry fees for the Finals
Winners qualify for DWC Finals in Spain (Note: Document mentions Italy for location but Spain for qualification)

Important Requirements

Dance schools must ensure routines meet DWC rules for choreography, costumes, and music
Dancers must compete in appropriate age category with age verification
Refer to DWC guidelines for detailed competition rules
